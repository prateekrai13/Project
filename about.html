ABOUT US

Our Past

Founded in 2020, Stray Animal Foundation of India already has improved the lives of multiple stray animals. We are shelter based rescue in Hyderabad city, India. Our grantees are Breath Animal Foster home – Hyderabad, India, Catty Tales – Mumbai, Guardians of Angels Trust – Haryana, Love for Animals Lord Budda Foundation – New Delhi, ALAI Rescues – Bangalore and numerous foster families. Working for Animals, Australia are our partners. Our volunteers bring into action; when we receive a report of a stray animal, our volunteers catch it and bring it to one of our shelter. Our shelter and foster staff then arrange for necessary veterinary and animal birth control procedures. Once recovered, they are released back where found or place them up for adoption. We’re working to get care, food and shelter for the stray animals of Hyderabad, India. Dozens of animals of all kinds given the care they need to survive. But we’re not done. The streets of Hyderabad in India are a rough place for stray animals, forced to fend for themselves against food scarcity, abuse, and temperatures up to 115 degrees. When I first saw the plight of these poor creatures, I knew I had to help. We formed our foundation to provide rescue crews, food, medical care, and training to give a brand new life to neglected dogs, cats, livestock and more. We’re thinking even bigger.
