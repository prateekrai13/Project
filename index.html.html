About Us

Our Mission

Happy Tails wish to give hope to Bhubaneswar’s street dogs and save them from being born into suffering.

Happy Tails foundation is a charitable organization working to build a stray dog welfare network.
Thousands of Pariah (Desi) dogs live on the streets of Bhubaneswar and sadly, sickness, injury and starvation kill most when they are just puppies.
With no government animal hospitals to help, too many suffer and die. We want to be their lifeline and help them in every way possible!

What do we do?

Rescue

We strive in and out for better lives of the unspeakable and inexpressible injured street dogs and in providing permanent home for paralysed and disabled dogs.

Vaccination

Karma foundation truly believes that the best way to reduce human - animal conflict & animal – animal conflict is birth control. We conduct ABC surgeries each day at our facility & keep the animal for Post Operative care until they are fit to be released back where they were picked up from.

Feeding Drives

Our OPD is a facility that is responsible for providing prevention, cure, and alleviation of diseases and pain. Our OPD clinic is a fully functional facility which is equipped to handle critical emergencies. We offer free treatment for stray animals at our OPD clinic.

Adoption

We aim to encourage more and more people to ADOPT, DON’T SHOP! We have many sweet, intelligent, loving and beautiful dogs waiting for their forever home.
